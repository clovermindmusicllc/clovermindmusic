About Clovermind Music

Clovermind Music is a production company and record label. Clovermind Music originally began as a funk trio in Navan Co.Meath Ireland, which is also where our logo originates just like it is carved into the stones at Newgrange Co.Meath. Our mission is to create, collaborate, and release forward-thinking sounds across the electronic spectrum.
