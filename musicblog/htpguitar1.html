Specs for the H1N

ORBA 2

It all began with YouTube. I spend hours on YouTube watching the latest music gear review videos. I was watching one of my favorite Youtubers Taetro, and in this particular video, he brought out the ORBA 2 — the latest MIDI device from Artiphon.

I was obsessed and I watched and consumed everything to do with the Orba — I needed to know everything about it. I was extremely lucky to get to visit the Artiphon booth at NAMM. Such a lovely team of people explained how the Orba also pairs over Bluetooth with an Orba application allowing you to change and alter the sounds.

The Orba offers a lot — way more than you would expect of a little orb. It consists of 8 pads which are all labeled. The first four pads are instruments and the second four offer effects such as Looper. This comes in handy to record your own beats on the fly. You know I love my portable equipment!

  • Capacitive Touch Sensitive Pads (8 pads with gestures: Tap, Press, Vibrato, Radiate, Slide)
  • Modulation and Controllers: Tilt, Spin, Shake, Bump, Move
  • Haptic Engine / Tactile feedback
  • Four-track MIDI Looper
  • Modes: Drum, Bass, Chord, Lead
  • Bluetooth LE MIDI
  • 3W built-in active speaker system
  • Companion App: iOS, Android, Mac, Windows

MONOTRON DELAY

The Monotron Delay is a monster pocket synth! This little guy comes with me everywhere. I can pair this with my ORBA 2 or simply run samples through it for some filter effects. I can even use the ribbon to create different transitions building up to the drops in my electronic music. Personally, it is a combo of my two favorite colors — black and orange. It is quite small and a little fragile, so I would recommend a case for this one. It runs on batteries and features two 1/8-inch jacks: one for input and one for output. It even has a small speaker installed so you can play with its functions and get an idea of which way you want it.

Synth structure: 1 VCO (Sawtooth Wave); 1 VCF; 1 LFO; DELAY
Switch: Standby / LFO Shape / LFO Shape
Knobs: LFO Rate, LFO Int., VCF Cutoff, Delay Time, Delay Feedback
Controller: Ribbon Controller
Audio Path: VCO, VCF, LFO
Inputs/Outputs: 1/8" stereo input & output
Power: 2× AAA batteries
Dimensions: 120×72×28mm
